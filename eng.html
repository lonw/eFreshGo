About

eFreshGo.com, Inc, often referred to as simply eFresh Go, is an Canadian e-commerce and food drivery company with headquarters in Markham, Toronto. It provides Door-to-Door Service in Toronto, Canada. The company was founded in 2016, spurred by Nancy Lee, who is also the founder of WELLY INTERNATIONAL MEDIA LTD. In 2016, she began to work on a business plan for what would eventually become efreshgo.com.
